our facilities

Playground

There are three adventure playgrounds which are challenging and safe play environments for children of all ages and abilities.

Lounge

One of our many facilities is a standard lounge where parents, visitors can sit and relax while waiting to pick their wards

KG Class

We boast of standard KG classrooms for our young learners. It is equipped with sinks, art tables and a broad range of equipment for creating visuals. Displays of children's work can be seen in the corridor outside the room.

First aid Station

We are situated in estates which boast of standard health centers within. Notwithstanding, we have a first aid station in the premises to provide first aid and care during emergencies and, also for minor injuries during sports or recess

ClassRoom

Airconditioned and heated classrooms, are complete with networked computers and interactive TV/whiteboards .

Cafeteria hall

Our cafeteria is newly opened for students to get their lunchs and breakfast from, its opened from Monday-Friday only.
