who we are

Higher Achievers' Group is the leading after school/tutoring program in Abuja, Nigeria and now has expanded to other parts of Nigeria, opening its first center in Abuja in 2011. We are very excited and proud to be the first Tutoring Center in Abuja, and feel that center is a perfect fit for students, parents and teachers in Nigeria who are looking for quality after-school tutoring services. Please feel free to contact us with any queries you might have, or drop by our office in any of our centers and meet our team!

Our Mission

The future is with us

We are committed to providing learners with high quality and individualized tutoring, empowering them to succeed in school. Through unique strategies, positive encouragement, and time and stress management skills, we empower learners to tackle whatever academic challenges may arise.
